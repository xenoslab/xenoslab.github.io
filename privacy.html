Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Information We Collect

  • Sign in with Apple or Google: Email address and display name (only what the sign-in provider shares with the app).
  • Game progress: Levels, points, Beans, and related gameplay data stored on your device.
  • In-app purchases: Purchases made through the Apple App Store. Payment is processed by Apple; the app does not store your card or payment details.

How We Use Information

  • To save game progress and link your account when you sign in.
  • To provide customer support.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and App Store purchases (on iOS).
  • Google — Sign in with Google (when you choose Google sign-in).

Storage and Deletion

  • Progress data is stored on your device and may be removed when you delete the app.
  • To request account or data deletion, contact: ryuharang@naver.com

Children

Hangul Pop is intended for general audiences learning Korean. If you believe we have collected a child’s personal information without appropriate consent, contact us at the email above.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect changes.
